About us

The event included fourteen matches that were evenly divided between the two nights. The matches resulted from scripted storylines, where wrestlers portrayed heroes, villains, or less distinguishable characters in scripted events that built tension and culminated in a wrestling match or series of matches. Results were predetermined by WWE's writers on the Raw and SmackDown brands,while storylines were produced on WWE's weekly television shows, Monday Night Raw and Friday Night SmackDown.
